About

Marga Garcia is a figurative artist specializing in oil portraiture and mixed media. Her work captures intimate moments of introspection, rendering her subjects with a classical sensitivity and a contemporary emotional depth.

Her paintings have been exhibited internationally, including at Toolip Art Gallery in Vienna — where she was awarded 1st Prize at the 4th Toolip International Art Contest in 2025 — and at the MEAM Museum in Barcelona.

Marga is also a finalist of The Almenara Art Prize. She continues to develop her practice from her studio in Barcelona, Spain.

Based in Barcelona, Spain
Medium Oil on canvas, mixed media
